## ğŸ“§ Contact Form Setup

The portfolio includes a fully functional contact form powered by **Formspree**.

### How to Set Up:

1. **Create a Formspree Account**
   - Visit [Formspree.io](https://formspree.io/)
   - Sign up for a free account

2. **Create a New Form**
   - Click "New Form" in your dashboard
   - Enter your email address
   - Copy the form endpoint URL

3. **Update the Contact Component**
   - Open `src/components/Contact.jsx` (or similar file)
   - Replace the form action with your Formspree endpoint:
   ```javascript
   const FORMSPREE_ENDPOINT = "https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID";
   ```

4. **Test the Form**
   - Fill out the contact form on your site
   - Check your email for the test message
